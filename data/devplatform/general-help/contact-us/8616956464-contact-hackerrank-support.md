# Contact DevPlatform Support

_Last updated: Sep 17, 2025, 10:05 PM (Last updated 7 months ago)_

If you need assistance with the DevPlatform for Work platform, use one of the following support options:

- **Submit a request:** Select **Support** in the footer of the DevPlatform for Work platform.

- **Email:** Send an email to [support@devplatform.com](mailto:support@devplatform.com).

**Note:** DevPlatform provides separate support options for Candidates and Community users:

- **Candidates:** Email [candidate-care@devplatform.com](mailto:candidate-care@devplatform.com)

- **Community users:** Visit the [DevPlatform Community Help Center](https://www.devplatform.com/support)
