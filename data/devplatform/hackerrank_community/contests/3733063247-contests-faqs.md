# Contests FAQs

_Last updated: Mar 12, 2025, 11:25 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

**Can I create and host coding contests or hackathons on the DevPlatform Community platform?**

Yes, DevPlatform Community platform allows you to host coding contests and hackathons. For more information, refer to the [📄 Introduction to Contests](/articles/7535813777).

**Does DevPlatform provide sponsorship for contests or hackathons?**

No, DevPlatform does not provide sponsorship for contests or hackathons.
