# Best Practices for Receiving DevPlatform Emails Check your spam, junk, or promotions folder Add DevPlatform to your contacts Verify mailbox storage

_Last updated: Oct 30, 2025, 3:05 PM (Last updated 6 months ago)_

Follow these best practices to ensure that emails from DevPlatform are successfully delivered to your inbox.

# Check your spam, junk, or promotions folder

If you do not see a DevPlatform email in your inbox:

- Check your spam, junk, or promotions folders.

- If you find the email, mark it as Not Spam or move it to your inbox.

This action helps your email provider recognize future emails from DevPlatform as safe.

# Add DevPlatform to your contacts

Add the following email addresses to your contacts or safe sender's list:

- [help@devplatform.com](mailto:support@devplatformforwork.com)

- [*@devplatform.com](mailto:no-reply@devplatform.com)

This action ensures that future communications from DevPlatform are delivered directly to your inbox.

# Verify mailbox storage

Make sure that your mailbox has sufficient storage space to receive new emails. A full mailbox can cause emails to bounce or fail to deliver.
