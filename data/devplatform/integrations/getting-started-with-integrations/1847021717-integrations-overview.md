# Integrations Overview

_Last updated: Mar 12, 2026, 2:56 PM (Last updated 1 month ago)_

DevPlatform provides seamless pre-defined integrations with several trending HR tools, including Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), Talent Intelligence, and Interview Scheduling tools. You can manage talent from one convenient location by assessing and interviewing candidates within your talent automation or intelligence platform. This ensures a streamlined hiring process and makes the recruiter workflow smooth.

DevPlatform supports these integrations based on the following types:

- [Applicant Tracking Systems](https://support.devplatform.com/articles/1847021717-integrations-overview#applicant-tracking-systems-4)\
  Embed DevPlatform into your recruiting process by accessing DevPlatform Tests and Interviews from your ATS.

- [Scheduling](https://support.devplatform.com/articles/1847021717-integrations-overview#scheduling-6)\
  Ease the technical interview scheduling by integrating DevPlatform Interviews with your scheduling tool.

- [Talent Intelligence](https://support.devplatform.com/articles/1847021717-integrations-overview#talent-intelligence-8)\
  Manage talent from one convenient location by assessing and interviewing candidates within your talent automation or intelligence platform.

- [Single Sign On](https://support.devplatform.com/articles/1847021717-integrations-overview#single-sign-on-10)\
  Keep your recruiting data secure and also ensure a seamless login to DevPlatform.

- [Productivity](https://support.devplatform.com/articles/1847021717-integrations-overview#productivity-12)\
  Automate your processes by connecting DevPlatform to your preferred productivity tools.
