# Prelude - DevPlatform Integration Guide

_Last updated: Jan 23, 2025, 3:25 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

DevPlatform integrates with Prelude for scheduling interviews. You can schedule DevPlatform interviews directly from Prelude as part of your interview process for single or loop interviews.

## Reaching Out for the Integration Help

To know more about how to integrate the Prelude with DevPlatform, you can reach out to the DevPlatform point of contact or raise a support request by writing to [support@devplatform.com](mailto:support@devplatform.com).

You can also refer to the [Prelude website](https://www.prelude.co/integrations/devplatform).
