# InterviewPlanner - DevPlatform Integration

_Last updated: Dec 24, 2024, 6:31 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

DevPlatform integrates with InterviewPlanner to facilitate a seamless interviewing experience to schedule remote Interviews from InterviewPlanner.\
\

Users can send DevPlatform Interview invites to the candidates.\
\

The procedure to configure the integration for InterviewPlanner with DevPlatform is as follows:

- [📄 Configuring InterviewPlanner - DevPlatform Integration](/articles/1300810624)
  \
- [📄 Using DevPlatform Interviews in InterviewPlanner](/articles/3675175160)
  \

You can also refer to the[📄 InterviewPlanner-DevPlatform Integration FAQs](/articles/7401637722) for troubleshooting.
