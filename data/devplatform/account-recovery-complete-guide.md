# DevPlatform Account Recovery — Complete Guide

## I Can't Log In

### Forgot Password
1. Go to devplatform.com/login
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Enter your registered email
4. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the reset link
5. The reset link expires after 24 hours

### Account Created via Social Login (Google, GitHub, LinkedIn)
If you signed up with a social login:
- You can still set a password: go to Login > "Forgot your password?" > enter your email
- This creates a standalone password alongside your social login
- Both login methods will work afterward

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Issues
If you're locked out due to 2FA:
- Use your backup codes (provided when you set up 2FA)
- If you've lost your backup codes, contact support with proof of identity
- Support can disable 2FA on your account within 24-48 hours after identity verification

### Account Compromised / Hacked
If you suspect unauthorized access:
1. Reset your password immediately using "Forgot your password?"
2. Enable 2FA if not already enabled
3. Check your account for unfamiliar activity (submissions, profile changes)
4. Contact support@devplatform.com with:
   - Your username
   - The email associated with your account
   - Description of suspicious activity
   - Approximate date you noticed the issue

**Important**: DevPlatform support cannot:
- Directly log into your account
- View your password (passwords are hashed)
- Transfer account ownership between users
- Merge two accounts into one

### Phone Number Recovery
If your recovery phone number has changed:
- You must contact support with proof of identity
- Acceptable proof: government-issued ID matching the name on the account, OR
- Verification through the registered email address
- Processing time: 2-5 business days

## I Want to Delete My Account

### DevPlatform Community
1. Log in to your account
2. Click your profile icon > Settings
3. Scroll to "Delete Account"
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Click "Delete Account"

**Warning**: This permanently deletes all your data, including:
- Profile and personal information
- Submissions and solved problems
- Badges and certifications
- Bookmarks and notes

### DevPlatform for Work (Enterprise)
Enterprise accounts cannot be self-deleted. Contact your organization's DevPlatform administrator.

## I Want to Merge Two Accounts

**Account merging is not currently supported.** Each DevPlatform account is independent.

If you have multiple accounts, we recommend:
- Choosing one account as your primary
- Deleting the other account(s)
- Note: Solved problems, badges, and history cannot be transferred between accounts
