# DevPlatform Educational and Nonprofit Pricing

## Academic Program

DevPlatform offers special pricing for educational institutions:

### University Program (Verified .edu email required)

| Plan | Standard Price | Academic Price | Discount |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter (up to 50 students) | $299/month | Free | 100% |
| Professional (up to 200 students) | $599/month | $149/month | 75% |
| Enterprise (unlimited) | Custom | Custom (40% off) | 40% |

### Coding Bootcamp Program

Accredited coding bootcamps and vocational training programs qualify for:
- 60% discount on Professional plans
- Free access to the question library
- Dedicated support channel

### Application Process

1. Visit devplatform.com/education
2. Submit your institution details and accreditation proof
3. A representative will contact you within 5 business days
4. If approved, you'll receive a promo code valid for 12 months

### Nonprofit Organizations

501(c)(3) organizations qualify for:
- 50% discount on all plans
- Proof of nonprofit status required (IRS determination letter)
- Annual renewal of discount
