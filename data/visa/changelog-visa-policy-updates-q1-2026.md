# CHANGELOG — Visa Support Policy Updates (Q1 2026)

## March 2026

### Zero Liability Policy — REVISED
**Effective March 15, 2026**, Visa's Zero Liability policy has been updated:

- Zero Liability now covers **all** transaction types including PIN-based transactions, ATM withdrawals, and wire transfers
- The previous exclusion for "gross negligence" (e.g., sharing your PIN) has been removed
- Cardholders are now covered even if they fail to report unauthorized transactions within 60 days
- Commercial card programs are now included (previously excluded)
- Prepaid cards are now included (previously limited coverage)

### Dispute Timeline — SHORTENED
- The dispute filing window has been reduced from **120 days to 90 days** from the transaction date
- Merchant response window reduced from 30 days to **15 days**
- Chargebacks are now automatically approved if the merchant fails to respond within 15 days

### Minimum Transaction Amounts — UPDATED
- Effective April 1, 2026, the maximum allowable minimum transaction amount has been **raised to $15** in the US (up from $10)
- This change aligns with the updated Dodd-Frank regulations signed in January 2026
- International minimums remain unchanged

## February 2026

### Emergency Services — EXPANDED
- Visa's emergency card replacement service now guarantees delivery within **24 hours worldwide** (previously 1-3 business days)
- Emergency cash advance limit increased to **$25,000** (previously $10,000)
- New: Free temporary virtual card number available immediately via the Visa app

## January 2026

### Cardholder Authentication — UPDATED
- 3-D Secure 2.0 is now mandatory for all online transactions over $50 (previously $100)
- Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) is now supported as a primary authentication method
- SMS OTP is being phased out as an authentication method by December 2026

---

*These changes apply globally unless otherwise noted. Regional implementations may vary.*
*For the most current information, visit visa.com/policies or contact your issuing bank.*
