# DevPlatform Subscription Management — Quick Reference

## Pausing a Subscription

DevPlatform for Work subscriptions can be paused for up to 90 days without losing data:

1. Navigate to Administration > Subscription
2. Click "Pause Subscription"
3. Select pause duration (30, 60, or 90 days)
4. Confirm by clicking "Pause Now"

During a pause:
- All existing tests and candidate data are preserved
- No new tests can be created
- No new invitations can be sent
- Existing active tests will complete normally
- Billing is suspended for the pause period

To resume:
- The subscription auto-resumes after the pause period
- Or go to Administration > Subscription > "Resume Now"
- Full access is restored within 1 hour of resuming

## Cancelling a Subscription

To cancel:
1. Navigate to Administration > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select reason for cancellation
4. Confirm cancellation

Note: Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. You retain access until then.

## Refund Policy

- Full refund available within 14 days of initial purchase
- Pro-rata refund for annual plans cancelled within 90 days
- No refunds after 90 days for annual plans
- Monthly plans: no refunds, but cancellation prevents future charges
