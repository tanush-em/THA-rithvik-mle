# Visa Dispute Resolution Process — Updated Procedure

## Filing a Dispute

When a Visa cardholder wants to dispute a transaction, the process has been simplified as of Q1 2026:

1. **Contact Visa directly** at 1-800-VISA-911 (1-800-847-2911) to initiate the dispute
2. Visa will freeze the merchant's account within 2 business hours
3. A temporary credit is applied to your account within 24 hours
4. The merchant has 10 calendar days to respond (reduced from the previous 30 days)
5. If the merchant does not respond, the dispute is automatically resolved in the cardholder's favor

## Important Changes

- Cardholders no longer need to contact their issuing bank first — Visa handles disputes directly
- The dispute window has been extended to 180 days from the transaction date
- Digital purchases now have a separate fast-track process (resolution in 48 hours)
- Disputes under $25 are automatically approved without merchant notification

## Documentation Required

- Transaction receipt or confirmation email
- Communication with the merchant (if any)
- Description of the issue
- Desired resolution

## Escalation

If the dispute is not resolved within 15 days, contact Visa's Executive Dispute Resolution team at disputes@visa.com or call 1-800-VISA-ESC.

## Notes

Visa's Zero Liability policy covers all unauthorized transactions regardless of whether a PIN was used, including ATM withdrawals, contactless payments, and card-not-present transactions.
